# 📊 Superstore Retail Data Analysis

Retail sales data analysis using Excel and Power BI, focusing on data cleaning, transformation, and deriving actionable insights for business decision-making.

---

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project analyzes a global retail superstore dataset spanning 4 years to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and operational efficiency. The dataset was sourced from Kaggle and processed using Excel for end-to-end data analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on retail sales data  
- Identify key revenue drivers and high-demand products  
- Analyze delivery performance and logistics efficiency  
- Understand customer purchasing patterns and repeat behavior  
- Support data-driven decision-making across business functions  

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
- **Source:** Kaggle  
- **Type:** Time-series retail dataset  
- **Duration:** 4 years  

### 📌 Features:
- Order Date, Ship Date  
- Customer ID, Segment, Region, City  
- Product Category, Sub-Category, Product Name  
- Sales and Order data  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Analysis)  
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)  

---

## 🔧 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Converted inconsistent date formats and corrected delivery calculations  
- Removed invalid records (e.g., incorrect shipping timelines)  
- Handled missing values and ensured data consistency  
- Standardized categorical fields for accurate grouping  
- Created derived metric: **Delivery Time (Ship Date – Order Date)**

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Revenue Driver:** Phones generate the highest sales, making them the primary revenue-driving product  
- **Demand Driver:** Binders have the highest order volume, indicating strong and consistent demand  
- **Market Concentration:** Major cities like New York and Los Angeles contribute significantly to total sales  
- **Operational Efficiency:** Delivery time ranges between 0–7 days, indicating efficient logistics performance  
- **Scalability Insight:** High order volumes do not significantly impact delivery time, reflecting a scalable supply chain  
- **Customer Behavior:** A small group of customers contributes disproportionately to total revenue, indicating high-value repeat customers  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🟢 Product Strategy
- Prioritize inventory for high-demand products (Binders)  
- Promote high-revenue products (Phones) to maximize profitability  

### 🔵 Customer Strategy
- Implement loyalty programs for repeat customers  
- Target high-value customers for retention and referrals  

### 🟡 Market Strategy
- Focus on high-performing cities for expansion and inventory allocation  

### 🔴 Operational Strategy
- Optimize delivery performance in slower regions  
- Maintain efficiency despite increasing order volume  

### 🟤 Product Optimization
- Promote or reevaluate low-performing product categories
